PPAC: >92% of India's LPG Imports From Gulf — All Now Disrupted
PPAC FY2024-25 data confirms: UAE supplies 40.5% of India's LPG imports, Qatar 22%, Saudi Arabia 15%, Kuwait contributing most of the remaining 15% — totalling over 92% from Gulf.
